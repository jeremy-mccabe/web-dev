A Brief Summary...

Illuminate is a web-based application designed to connect people with valuable skills and know-how that require valuable time and resources to learn. We've long lost the art of teaching others our trade free of cost, and Illuminate acts to fill that gap. The monetary system can place limits on what we have the finances to learn. Building bridges of communication and knowledge brings us closer together as a community, and offers abilities that were otherwise out of reach. This is a free avenue of trade, and our services are without cost. All you give is your time.
